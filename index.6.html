I love color

Color or colour is the visual perception based on the electromagnetic spectrum.

Blue

Blue is one of the three primary colours in the RYB colour model The term blue generally describes colours perceived by humans observing light with a dominant wavelength between approximately 450 and 495 nanometres.

Green

Green is the color between cyan and yellow on the visible spectrum. It is evoked by light which has a dominant wavelength of roughly 495–570 nm. Green is common in nature,which is involved in photosynthesis.

Red
